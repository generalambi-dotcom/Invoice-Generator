### Client Management
- [ ] Save and manage client database
- [ ] Quick client selection from dropdown
- [ ] Client contact history
- [ ] Client notes and tags

### Invoice Numbering
- [ ] Automatic invoice numbering
- [ ] Customizable number format (e.g., INV-2024-001)
